# Code of Conduct

EBSL should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction quickly.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear reproduction steps and focused proposals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Encouraging misuse of the project against server rules, platform rules, or other players.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject contributions; or block participants when needed to protect the project.

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.
